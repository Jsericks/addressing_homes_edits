THE PLAN

It has taken 7 years to develop the software that will be used to create a national addressing system. Because Liberia was the inspiration for the program, it will also act as the pilot. Upon the successful launch of Addressing Homes in Liberia, the program will be offered to countries around the world.

Our plan is to change the world. We will start by making the lives of the people in Liberia a little bit better.
